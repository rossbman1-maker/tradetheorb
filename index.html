Frequently Asked Questions

Is this suitable if I’m a complete beginner?
Yes. Level 1 of the academy is designed exactly for you — we start with how the markets work, what brokers do, what leverage actually means in practice, and how risk, news and price action fit together. You don’t need prior trading experience, just the willingness to learn and put in the work.
What if I already have some trading experience?
Many traders come in with some background (watching YouTube, trying a few strategies, maybe trading live already). You can move quickly through the foundational modules and then dive into the ORB system, case studies and prop firm material. The value is in having everything structured in one place with a clear framework to follow.
Does this guarantee that I’ll pass a prop firm challenge?
No. No ethical educator can guarantee specific trading results. Markets carry risk and your outcomes depend on factors I can’t control: your discipline, psychology, risk choices and consistency. What I can do is give you a structured framework, education, tools and a realistic blueprint to improve your odds and reduce common mistakes.
How long do I have access to the material?
As a founding member, you get lifetime access. That means you pay once, and you can come back to the videos, PDFs and future updates at any time, at your own pace.
Will there be live sessions or a community?
The core academy is a self-paced course. Depending on demand and the number of founding members, I may add live Q&A calls or a private community (e.g., Discord) as a separate or bundled offering. If/when that happens, founding members will get first access and preferential pricing.
